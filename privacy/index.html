Hako Privacy Policy

Personal Information

Hako’s app developer cares deeply about your privacy. Hako doesn’t collect any personal information.

Analytics

Hako uses TelemetryDeck to collect anonymous usage data, such as app launches. By using Hako, you are subject to TelemetryDeck’s Privacy Policy. Apple may also send anonymous analytics information to the developer.

RevenueCat

Hako uses RevenueCat to manage subscriptions. By using Hako, you are subject to RevenueCat’s Privacy Policy.

Updates

This policy may be updated at any time. You should check this page occasionally to ensure you are familiar with any changes.
